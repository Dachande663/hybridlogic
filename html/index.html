My name is Luke Lanchester, a web developer & geek from the heart of England. As a principal engineer at 383, an award-winning digital products and services company, I design and build applications used by millions of people every month.

Companies I have worked with include the world’s largest hotel chain, Hilton Worldwide; the oldest national broadcaster, the BBC and the FTSE-100 multi-billion dollar investment company Hammerson PLC, to name a few.

My own projects include Bulk Buffer, WP Slab, GPS Heatmaps and many more. Together they have been used and downloaded millions of times, and all released for free to the community.
